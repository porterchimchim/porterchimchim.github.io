Here Are My Thoughts About Life!

Technology

Technology is a dangerous thing. I think we as a society hardly realize how harmful our incredible innovations can be.

I have noticed from personal experience that the internet and our hand-held devices give people a sense of power, that there are no rules to follow.

People use this to harm others, in ways we never thought immaginable. Is it possible that we ignore these issues because we know they're real?

Writing
